Gmail One-Click Cleaner - Rules

One Gmail search query per line. These rule sets define what gets cleaned for each level. Unsaved changes

In live runs (not Dry-Run), matching conversations are first tagged with labels like GmailCleaner - Promotions or GmailCleaner - Big attachments so you can search and audit them later. In Safe Mode, rules that include category:updates or category:forums are automatically skipped. Global guardrails such as skip starred and skip important are layered on top of whatever you put here.

Protection

Emails or domains entered here (one per line, e.g. boss@work.com or *@family.com) are never touched. The cleaner will automatically append -from:... to every search.

Protected Keywords (Never Delete)

Protection

Words or phrases entered here (one per line, e.g. tax, invoice, or "flight confirmation") protect any message whose subject contains them from every rule. The cleaner appends -subject:(...) to each search. Applies to manual and scheduled runs.
