Gmail One-Click Cleaner – Rules

One Gmail search query per line. These rule sets define what gets cleaned for each level. Unsaved changes

ℹ️ In live runs (not Dry-Run), matching conversations are first tagged with labels like GmailCleaner - Promotions or GmailCleaner - Big attachments so you can search and audit them later. In Safe Mode, rules that include category:updates or category:forums are automatically skipped. Global guardrails such as skip starred and skip important are layered on top of whatever you put here.
