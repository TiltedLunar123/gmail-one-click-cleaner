🐛 Debug mode & console logs

advanced

The cleaner can emit structured debug logs (prefixed with [GmailCleaner]) to your browser console to help track down why a run paused, skipped, throttled, or stopped early.

  1. Open Gmail in a tab and start a run from the popup.
  2. Press Ctrl+Shift+J (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Option+J (macOS) to open DevTools.
  3. Filter the console by [GmailCleaner] to see timing, query, and error details.

🔒 Logs never leave your browser.
